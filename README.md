# Clinical Safety Risk Flag Tool

**Clinically informed decision support prototype for identifying silent patient safety risks, informed by contemporary patient safety and early warning guidelines.**

---

## Overview
This project is a prototype clinical decision support tool designed to demonstrate how silent and cumulative patient safety risks can be identified early using simple, explainable, rule-based logic.

The tool aims to support healthcare teams by making subtle risk patterns more visible and actionable, without replacing clinical judgment or local protocols.

---

## Key Features
- Risk stratification: Low risk / Monitor closely / Escalation recommended
- Explainable, rule-based decision logic
- Action-oriented clinical recommendations
- Clinically informed thresholds aligned with contemporary safety guidance
- Lightweight, web-based prototype

---

## Intended Use
This prototype is developed for innovation, demonstration, and educational purposes only.  
It is **not intended for direct clinical use** or to replace professional judgment.
